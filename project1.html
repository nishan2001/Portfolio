Depop

The Depop Clothing Web project involved creating an engaging and user-friendly e-commerce platform for fashion enthusiasts who seek to buy and sell second-hand clothing. Inspired by the popular Depop app, the website aimed to replicate its trendy, social-commerce experience while enhancing it with additional features and customization. Key Features: User Profiles: Users can create and customize their profiles, showcase their collections, and interact with other users. Product Listings: An intuitive interface for users to list their items for sale, complete with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and pricing. Search and Filters: Advanced search functionality and filtering options to help users easily find specific items or browse by categories. Social Integration: Features that allow users to follow others, like and comment on listings, and share their favorite finds on social media. Secure Transactions: A streamlined checkout process with secure payment options and order tracking.
