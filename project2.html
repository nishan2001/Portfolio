Gloria Foods

The Gloria Foods project focused on creating a sleek and user-friendly website for a food delivery service. The site was designed to streamline the ordering process and enhance the customer experience with a clear, intuitive interface. Key features include: Online Menu: A detailed and visually appealing menu with real-time updates on item availability. Order Management: A seamless ordering system with options for delivery and pickup, including customization of orders. User Accounts: Personalized user profiles allowing for order history tracking, saved favorites, and easy reordering. Responsive Design: A mobile-friendly layout ensuring accessibility across all devices.
